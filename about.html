About

The Studio

CelesiumAI is a Houston-based AI development studio that designs, builds, and operates purpose-built artificial intelligence platforms. We are the technology arm of Celesium Capital — a private equity firm with deep roots in healthcare, technology, and real estate investment.

We don't build AI for the sake of AI. Every engine we create solves a specific, high-value problem — whether that's detecting medical billing fraud, screening research security risks, or evaluating the commercial viability of academic IP.

Our approach is rooted in the belief that the best AI products are built by teams who understand the domain as deeply as they understand the technology.
